How it works

Three steps. Zero guesswork.

1
Connect

Your ring talks to YU.

One tap connects your Oura Ring or Apple Watch. YU instantly reads 170+ days of sleep, HRV, readiness, and stress data. No CSV exports. No manual entry.

2
Train

AI writes your session.

Based on your readiness, recovery state, and training history — YU generates the exact workout for today. Structure, loads, movements, rest times. Then validates everything.

3
Adapt

Tomorrow adjusts automatically.

Your next-morning HRV tells YU whether yesterday's session was too much, just right, or not enough. After 30 sessions, it knows your body better than any coach.

The System

Not an app. A training OS.

Reads your body

HRV, readiness, sleep, heart rate, respiratory rate, stress — all from your Oura Ring or Apple Watch.

Programs your workout

AI picks structure, loads, and movements. A validator checks every exercise against your equipment.

Gets harder as you do

Hit the prescribed weight twice cleanly — it bumps. Grind at RPE 10 — it holds. Your progression.

Catches overtraining

Four biometric signals vs your baselines. If your body needs rest, the system forces it.

Learns your patterns

Tracks your streak, skip patterns, and preferred training time. Nudges when you're most likely to show up.

Closes the loop daily

Tomorrow's HRV judges yesterday's workout. That verdict shapes the next session. Every day sharper.
