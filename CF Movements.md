# Omar – At‑Home CrossFit Movement Catalog
Equipment: Pair DBs (35/40/50 lb), treadmill, strict pull-up bar  
Rule: Lunges ONLY bodyweight. Everything else: go all‑in.

---

## 1. Squat Patterns (Loaded & Unloaded)

**1.1 Bodyweight Squats**

- Air squat  
  - Feet shoulder-width.  
  - Hips descend until hip crease clearly below top of knee, stand to full hip/knee extension. [web:16][web:25]  

- Tempo squat  
  - 3–5 seconds down, 1–2 second pause, fast up, same depth standard.  

- Jump squat  
  - Full squat, then jump so both feet leave floor, land with soft knees, maintain squat standard.  

- Narrow-stance squat  
  - Feet under hips, forces more quad.  

- Sumo squat  
  - Wide stance, toes slightly out, squat below parallel.

---

**1.2 Goblet & Front-Loaded Squats (DB)**

- Goblet squat (single DB)  
  - DB held vertically at chest (both hands).  
  - Squat below parallel, stand to full extension. [web:17][web:22]  

- Double DB front squat  
  - DBs in front rack (heads on shoulders or just above, elbows slightly forward).  
  - Full squat depth, full extension at top. [web:16][web:22]  

- Single-arm front rack squat  
  - One DB in front rack, other arm free or counterbalanced.  

---

**1.3 Back-Loaded & Overhead Squats (DB)**

- DB back squat (improvised)  
  - DBs resting on upper traps/shoulders.  
  - Same depth standard, brace hard.

- Double DB overhead squat  
  - DBs locked out overhead, biceps near ears, stable shoulders.  
  - Full depth squat while maintaining overhead position. [web:16]  

- Single-arm overhead squat  
  - One DB overhead, other arm for balance.

- Zercher DB squat (cradled in elbows)  
  - One or two DBs in elbow pits at chest level.

---

**1.4 Squat + Pattern Combos**

- Squat to press (front squat into strict press, slower than thruster)  
- Squat to curl (front squat, stand, DB curl)  
- Tempo thruster (slow eccentric on squat, fast press out)

---

## 2. Lunge Patterns (Bodyweight Only)

*All lunge variations: knee kisses/approaches floor under hip, torso mostly upright, full extension at top.*

- Forward lunge (in place)  
- Reverse lunge  
- Walking lunge (space permitting)  
- Lateral lunge  
- Curtsy lunge  
- Split squat (stationary, front foot planted)  
- Bulgarian split squat (rear foot elevated on box/bench)  
- Jump lunge (switch lunge)  
- Skater jump (lateral bound with soft landing)

No added load to respect your lunge rule.

---

## 3. Hinge / Deadlift Patterns (DB)

**3.1 Deadlifts**

- DB deadlift (sumo or conventional)  
  - DBs start on floor (both heads touching or close), neutral spine.  
  - Stand until hips and knees fully extended. [web:16][web:17]  

- DB Romanian deadlift (RDL)  
  - Soft knees, hinge at hips, DBs close to legs, tension in hamstrings.

- Staggered-stance DB deadlift  
  - One foot slightly back, more single-leg loading.

- Single-leg DB RDL  
  - DB in ipsilateral or contralateral hand, hinge on one leg.

---

**3.2 Hip / Posterior Chain Accessories**

- Glute bridge (BW)  
- Single-leg glute bridge  
- Hip thrust (BW, shoulders elevated)  
- Hip thrust + abduction (band if you add one later)

---

## 4. Horizontal Pressing (Floor & Plank)

**4.1 Push-Ups**

Standard push-up  
- High plank, straight line shoulders–hips–heels.  
- Lower until chest nearly/briefly touches floor while thighs stay off, press to full elbow lockout. [web:26]  

Variations:  
- Incline push-up (hands elevated)  
- Decline push-up (feet elevated)  
- Diamond / narrow-grip push-up  
- Wide-grip push-up  
- Archer push-up  
- Hand-release push-up (lift hands briefly at bottom)  
- Plyometric / clapping push-up  
- Shoulder tap push-up (tap opposite shoulder at top)  

---

**4.2 DB Floor Pressing**

- DB floor press  
  - Lying on back, DBs over chest.  
  - Lower until triceps contact floor, press to full lockout. [web:17][web:21]  

- Alternating DB floor press  
- Single-arm DB floor press  
- Glute-bridge DB floor press (hips extended)  
- Neutral-grip DB floor press (palms facing each other)

---

## 5. Vertical & Angled Pressing (DB)

**5.1 Standing / Seated Vertical Press**

- DB strict press  
  - DBs at shoulders, press overhead until elbows locked, biceps by ears, no leg drive. [web:16][web:27]  

- DB push press  
  - Dip, drive, and press, finishing with hips, knees, elbows fully extended. [web:16]  

- DB push jerk  
  - Dip and drive, then re-dip under DBs and stand to complete.

- Single-arm strict press / push press / push jerk  
- Seated DB press (on floor, legs straight or butterfly)

---

**5.2 Angled / Accessory Pressing**

- Arnold press  
- Z-press (seated on floor, legs straight, DB strict press)  
- DB Cuban press (external rotation into press, lighter load)  
- DB lateral raise  
- DB front raise  
- DB Y-raise (prone or bent-over for scap health)

---

## 6. Pulling – Vertical (Bar + DB)

**6.1 Strict Pull-Up Standards**

- Strict pull-up  
  - Dead hang with elbows locked and shoulders relaxed.  
  - Pull until chin clearly breaks horizontal plane of bar, control down to full extension. [web:6][web:24][web:26]  

- Strict chin-up (supinated grip)  
- Mixed-grip pull-up  
- Tempo pull-up (slow eccentric)  
- Paused pull-up (pause at 90° or top)  
- Weighted pull-up (DB between feet or legs)

**6.2 Other Vertical Pulls**

- Strict chest-to-bar pull-up  
  - Same start, chest contacts bar at or below collarbone. [web:6]  

- Mixed-grip chest-to-bar  
- Isometric top hold (chin or chest above bar)  
- Isometric mid-hold (90° elbows)  
- Active hang (scap depression/retraction, no elbow bend)

**6.3 DB Vertical Pull / Accessories**

- DB pull-over (on floor)  
  - Hips bridged or flat, DB travels overhead and back in an arc.  

- Single-arm DB high pull to eye-level (controlled, not a snatch)

---

## 7. Pulling – Horizontal (DB & Bodyweight)

- Bent-over DB row (bilateral)  
  - Hinge, neutral spine, pull DBs to lower ribs, elbows behind torso. [web:18]  

- Single-arm DB row (supported on leg or box)  
- Pendlay-style DB row (from dead stop off floor each rep)  
- Chest-supported DB row (lying on bench/box if you add later)  
- DB reverse fly  
- DB seal row (lying on something with chest supported, DBs under body)

---

## 8. Core & Midline (Floor + Hanging)

**8.1 Floor Core**

- Sit-up (standard)  
- Butterfly sit-up (feet together)  
- V-up  
- Tuck-up  
- Dead bug  
- Hollow hold  
- Hollow rock  
- Arch hold (Superman)  
- Russian twist (bodyweight or with DB)  
- DB weighted sit-up (DB at chest or overhead)  
- DB side bend (standing, single DB)  

**8.2 Plank Variations**

- Front plank on elbows or hands  
- Side plank  
- Plank with shoulder taps  
- Plank DB drag (drag a DB from side to side under body)  
- Plank DB reach (reach DB forward and back)  

**8.3 Hanging Core (Bar)**

- Hanging knee raises (strict, no kip)  
- Hanging leg raises  
- L-hang / L-sit (if bar height allows)  
- Tuck hold  
- Alternating knee-to-elbow (strict)  

---

## 9. DB Olympic / Power Patterns (Clean, Snatch, Complexes)

**9.1 Cleans**

- DB power clean (from floor)  
  - DBs start on floor, hips and knees extend, DBs travel to shoulders, catch above parallel. [web:16][web:17]  

- DB hang power clean  
  - From hang (above knee), extend and catch at shoulders above parallel. [web:16]  

- DB squat clean (from floor)  
  - Clean then ride into full squat, stand to finish.  

- DB hang squat clean  
- Single-arm DB power clean  
- Single-arm DB squat clean  

---

**9.2 Snatches**

- Single-arm DB power snatch (from floor)  
  - DB starts on floor, one motion to overhead, hips/knees/elbow fully extended at top. [web:16][web:17]  

- Alternating DB snatch  
  - Switch hands below face or at lower position per standard. [web:16]  

- DB hang power snatch  
- DB hang squat snatch (technical)  
- Dual-DB snatch (both DBs from floor to overhead)  

---

**9.3 Complexes & Hybrids**

- Man Maker (push-up + rows + clean + thruster)  
  - Hands on DBs, push-up (chest to floor), one row each side, jump feet in, clean DBs to shoulders, full squat, then press to overhead in one motion. [web:20][web:22]  

- Devil press  
  - Burpee with hands on DBs, then swing/clean DBs from floor to overhead in one motion, full extension at top. [web:20]  

- Burpee DB clean (no press)  
- Burpee DB deadlift  
- “Cluster” (DB squat clean thruster)  

---

## 10. DB Holds (Isometric)

- Static DB hold at top of deadlift  
- Static goblet hold in squat (isometric)

---

## 11. Burpees & Burpee Hybrids

- Standard burpee  
  - Hands to floor, jump back, chest and thighs touch floor, jump up with full hip/knee extension, small jump and clap/overhead reach. [web:16][web:25]  

- Burpee to strict pull-up  
- Burpee pull-up (jump to bar, strict pull-up)  
- Lateral burpee over DB  
- Bar-facing-style burpee to an object (imaginary line or DB)  
- Burpee broad jump  
- No-push-up burpee (sprawl variant for volume days)  

---

## 12. Running / Treadmill

- Easy run (Z2 style)  
- Threshold intervals (e.g. 800m / 1k repeats)  
- Sprint intervals (short 100–200m efforts)  
- Incline hiking / uphill intervals  
- Treadmill “chipper” segments (fixed distance between DB/bar blocks)

---

## 13. Lower-Body Plyometrics (No Extra Equipment)

- Box jump substitute:  
  - Tuck jumps  
  - Broad jumps  
  - Repeated squat jumps  

- Skater jumps (lateral bounds)  
- Single-leg pogo hops (ankle stiffness work)

## 14. Shoulder Health / Prehab (DB + BW)

- Scap push-up (plus)  
- Scap pull-up (active hang plus)  
- DB external rotation (elbow on knee or side)  
- DB Cuban press (high pull + rotation + press, very light)  
- DB “reverse fly T/Y/W” in hinge  
- Band work in the future if you add one (face pulls, pull-aparts) [web:27]
